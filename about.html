BIOGRAPHY

Heart Angela Uy, a renowned Filipino dancer, gained prominence as
a member of Vpeepz in 2018. She also garnered recognition
through her participation in the dance competition "World of Dance."
Born on October 29, 2004, in Manila, Philippines, Heart Angela
Uy had previously been affiliated with Lil Peepz. In 2015,
she attained her brown belt in Taekwondo, showcasing her multifaceted
interests in the world of sports. Nevertheless, Heart Angela Uy
unwaveringly pursued her true passion, which was dancing. Tragically, in
2023, she passed away at the age of 18.
